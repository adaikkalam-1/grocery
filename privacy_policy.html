01

Information We Collect

At Groca, we collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Data: Transaction details when you make a purchase (handled securely via encrypted gateways).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, and interaction with our platform.
02

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us to:

Process and deliver orders.
Personalize your experience.
Send service updates.
Improve site performance.
03

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze site traffic.

Note: You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly.
04

Security & Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

Data is stored only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements.

05

Your Privacy Rights

Under privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Object to processing for marketing purposes.
06

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted partners (like delivery services and payment processors) essential to fulfilling your orders.
